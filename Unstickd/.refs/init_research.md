## 1. Executive Summary

**Unstickd** is an evidence-based, AI-augmented writing environment for children (ages 6‚Äì12) that prioritizes the **creative process over the final product**. Unlike "Ghostwriting" AI tools that generate text for the user, Unstickd utilizes **Passive Socratic Intervention** to keep the child in the "Zone of Proximal Development."

The business employs a **B2B2C strategy**: offering the tool at-cost to school divisions as a teacher-augmentation utility, while monetizing through a premium home-tier subscription focused on digital continuity and physical keepsake publishing.

## 2. The Core Problem & Solution

| The Problem | The Unstickd Solution |
| --- | --- |
| **Academic Integrity:** AI tools often "ghostwrite," leading to learning loss and school bans. | **Zero-Ghostwriting Policy:** Hard architectural constraints prevent the AI from writing into the body text. |
