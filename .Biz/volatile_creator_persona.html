Composite Design Mandates

1. The "Valve" Mechanism

Problem: It's not a lack of ideas (writer's block); it's a flood (traffic jam).
Solution: The AI acts as a Valve, isolating ONE variable at a time ("Just pick the setting first") to prevent cognitive washout.

2. The "Fire" Needs a Fireplace

Problem: Once the valve opens, the ideas rush out without structure.
Solution: The tool acts as the Narrative Container. It must hold the plot points (Beginning, Middle, End) visible at all times so he can't get lost.

3. Voice > Typing

Problem: Hand speed < Brain speed.
Solution: A Voice-First Interface captures the manic burst of ideas, then the AI transcribes and organizes them for him to review calmly.
