The "Incompetence" Friction

She fears looking helpless. If the tool requires troubleshooting, she will abandon it to save face.
Design Rule: Speak to her in plain language and deliver zero setup accounts.

The "Real Work" Bias

She views digital work as lazy. She needs proof of effort.
Design Rule: Tools need to teach a system, not just create the result.

The Hostage User

She isn't looking to be delighted; she wants to be done.
Design Rule: Least possible supervision needed.
